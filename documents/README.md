# Documents – NVO987 Reference Library

This directory contains reference documents used by the NVO987 research infrastructure website.

The materials collected here serve educational and informational purposes and represent internationally recognized frameworks related to:

- human rights
- cultural diversity
- knowledge development
- research ethics
- open cultural participation

These documents are not modified and remain the intellectual property of their respective organizations.

## Included references

### Universal Declaration of Human Rights
Adopted by the United Nations General Assembly on 10 December 1948.

Source: United Nations

Purpose on this site:  
Provides the fundamental ethical reference for human dignity, freedom of expression, and cultural participation.

### UNESCO Universal Declaration on Cultural Diversity
Adopted by UNESCO in 2001.

Source: UNESCO

Purpose on this site:  
Highlights the importance of cultural plurality, intercultural dialogue, and preservation of cultural heritage.

## Usage

The files in this directory are provided for:

- educational reference

- research infrastructure documentation

They are linked from the NVO987 public website.
