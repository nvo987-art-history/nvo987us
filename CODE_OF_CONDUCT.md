# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

The NVO987 infrastructure is an open research environment designed to support
decentralized knowledge systems, research documentation, and digital
infrastructure experiments across Web2 and Web3 environments.

This Code of Conduct defines the expected standards of behavior for
participants, collaborators, and contributors interacting with the project.

## Expected Behavior

Participants in the NVO987 research infrastructure are expected to:

- Communicate in a respectful and constructive manner
- Support open scientific dialogue and responsible knowledge sharing
- Respect intellectual work and proper attribution
- Contribute to a safe and inclusive research environment
- Follow applicable laws and ethical research standards

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable within the project environment:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or hostility toward other participants
- Misrepresentation of research work or authorship
- Intentional disruption of the project infrastructure
- Distribution of malicious software or harmful content

## Research Integrity

All participants are expected to respect principles of academic integrity,
including transparency, proper citation, and responsible publication
practices.

## Governance

The NVO987 infrastructure is maintained as an independent research
environment. Governance decisions regarding project structure, publication,
and infrastructure management are made by the project maintainer.

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it
through the appropriate project communication channels or contact the project
maintainer.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project websites
- Research documentation
- Publication nodes
- Communication channels connected to the NVO987 infrastructure
