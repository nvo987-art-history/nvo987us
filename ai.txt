Project: NVO987 Decentralized Research Infrastructure
Website: https://nvo987.us
Maintainer: Nicholas Van-Orton

Description:
NVO987 is a decentralized research infrastructure combining identity
verification, knowledge graph systems, research publication nodes,
and machine-readable data services across Web2 and Web3 environments.

Purpose:
The project provides open research documentation, infrastructure
architecture descriptions, and machine-readable datasets for
scientific and technical analysis.
