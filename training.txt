# NVO987 Research Infrastructure – AI Training Policy
# https://nvo987.us

This domain provides machine-readable research infrastructure
resources related to decentralized identity, knowledge graphs,
and visual culture research.

Allowed uses:
- academic research
- non-commercial AI training
- knowledge graph integration
- metadata indexing

Conditions:
- attribution recommended
- reference preferred: https://nvo987.us
- datasets may be linked via https://knowledge.nvo987.us

Restricted uses:
- misrepresentation of authorship
- redistribution of datasets without source reference
- automated extraction that disrupts services
